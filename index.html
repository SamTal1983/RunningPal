Run Analysis

You will be able to keep track of the work you've done by distance you ran. We will save your data in our database, and when you'll run that distance again, we will provide you with statistics, graph, the difference between the runs and all previous relevant runs. You'll be able to see for yourself how much better you're doing!

Shoes Management

Let us help you manage your shoes. We will color-code your shoes based on your distance:
Green: 0-149.99 miles
Yellow: 150-249.99 miles
Red: 250 miles and up
Flashing Red: above 500 miles
Blue: unused shoes for a period of over 6 months, potential for retirement
We are here for your healthy feet!

Your Intervals

Let's set up your intervals the way you want to set it up. You'll be able to name up to 10 different paces and set time for them; you'll also be able to edit them, just in case you want something else. You can set up a total time for the intervals or just let them run in a loop (we'll create that loop for you) until you'll manually stop the clock. However you want it, you can have it! Then, get the RunningPal app on your phone, log in to your account choose your interval plan, and start your run. We've got you covered!
